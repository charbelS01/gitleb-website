The firm behind the systems.

GitLeb is an independent digital transformation firm in Jbeil, Lebanon. We take companies from strategy on paper to systems in production, and we stay accountable for both.

Our story

Built on a simple observation

Technology projects rarely fail on code. They fail in the gaps: between the advisor and the builder, between the deadline and the corner that got cut, between what was promised and what was scoped.

So we closed the gaps. Every engagement starts with clear scoping and an honest timeline. You see real progress every week, not a status report. Work is reviewed, tested and shipped continuously, so feedback lands while change is still cheap.

From Jbeil we work with owners, executives and product teams across MENA and Europe. Whether you need a transformation roadmap, a BI platform your board actually reads, a core system replacement that lands, or software that simply works, one firm carries it end to end.
