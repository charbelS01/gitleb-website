GitLeb is an independent Jbeil studio engineering websites, custom software and automation systems for businesses that care about quality. We move fast without cutting corners — and ship work we're proud to put our name on.

Our Story

Built around how good software actually ships.

Most software projects don't fail because of bad code — they fail because of poor communication, shifting scope and corners cut to make a deadline. We built GitLeb to do it differently.

Every engagement starts with clear scoping and an honest timeline. You see real progress every week, not a status report. Code is reviewed, tested and shipped continuously, so feedback happens early — while change is still cheap and the work stays on track.

From our base in Jbeil we work with founders, product teams and operators across MENA and Europe. Whether you need a website that converts, a custom platform that scales, or workflows that just work — we'll deliver it end-to-end.
