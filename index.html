About GitLeb

Engineering teams that actually deliver.

GitLeb is an independent Beirut studio engineering websites, custom software and automation systems for ambitious businesses. We work in tight, focused teams — the people who scope your project are the people who build it.

We pair the craft and care of a boutique studio with the discipline of a much larger one: clear scoping, honest timelines, regular visible progress, and software you can trust in production.
