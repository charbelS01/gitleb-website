01

Digital Transformation Advisory

We take ownership of transformation the way an in-house digital chief would: group strategy, technology roadmaps and operating models that match how the business actually grows. We assess, we plan, and then we stay to deliver.

Strategy & roadmaps · Operating models · Vendor governance · Change management

  • Digital maturity assessment

    A structured review of systems, data and processes. The deliverable is a prioritized, costed roadmap, not a binder.

  • Transformation programs

    Multi-workstream programs across core systems, data and process, planned, governed and executed with clear ownership.

  • IT governance & vendor oversight

    Operating models, KPIs and vendor management that keep technology accountable to the business.

02

Business Intelligence & Analytics

Turn scattered operational data into decisions. We build the full BI stack: data models, pipelines, KPI frameworks and dashboards your leadership actually uses. We have stood up BI functions in banking and global logistics, and we bring that playbook to your business.

Power BI · Qlik · SQL · Python · ETL pipelines

  • Executive dashboards & KPI frameworks

    A structured KPI model per function, delivered as live dashboards rather than another report nobody opens.

  • Data warehousing & pipelines

    Clean, scheduled, monitored data flows from your operational systems into one trusted source.

  • Advanced analytics

    Segmentation, churn and forecasting models. Statistics applied to business questions, not for show.

03

Master Data Management

One version of the truth across every system. We design and implement MDM platforms on certified enterprise tooling: golden records, data models, stewardship workflows and integrations, delivered to the standard regulated industries demand.

Tibco EBX (certified) · Golden records · Data modeling · Integration

  • MDM implementation

    Platform setup, data models, match-and-merge rules and workflows, from pilot domain to group rollout.

  • Integration & migration

    Master data flowing cleanly between your core platforms, CRM and operational systems, with quality gates at every boundary.

  • Stewardship operating model

    Ownership, approval flows and quality metrics so master data stays clean after we leave.

04

Systems Integration & Modernization

Companies rarely suffer from too few systems. They suffer from systems that do not talk. We modernize the core: platform selection, replacement of aging applications, and the integration layer that keeps orders, inventory and finance telling the same story.

Platform selection · Legacy replacement · Integration architecture · Data migration

  • Platform selection & vendor governance

    Requirements, shortlists and negotiation support from your side of the table. We hold vendors to the scope they sold.

  • Integration architecture

    APIs, event flows and reconciliation between your core platforms, so data moves once and moves correctly.

  • Legacy migration & cut-over

    Old systems retired on a plan: data mapped, migrated and verified, with the cut-over rehearsed before it happens.

05

Financial Systems Consulting

Specialist support for the platforms banks and treasuries run on. We advise on capital markets and treasury system implementations: technical design, environment architecture, performance and security hardening, and go-live support, with experience serving tier-1 EMEA institutions.

Capital markets platforms · Technical design authority · Performance tuning · Java · Oracle · MSSQL

  • Implementation & upgrade support

    Technical stream leadership from scoping and design through build, UAT and go-live.

  • Architecture & sizing

    Infrastructure sizing, operability and manageability reviews before problems reach production.

  • Performance & security hardening

    Tuning, penetration-test remediation and environment stability for systems that cannot go down.

06

Data Governance & Compliance

Data you can trust, defend and audit. We design and deploy governance frameworks covering master data, quality rules, ownership models and regulatory alignment, sized for multi-entity groups without the big-four bureaucracy.

Data quality · Ownership models · Regulatory compliance · Access governance

  • Governance frameworks

    Ownership, stewardship and quality rules per data domain. Pragmatic, not bureaucratic.

  • Master data control

    One version of customers, products and vendors across every system that touches them.

  • Compliance readiness

    Audit trails, retention and access controls aligned with regulatory requirements.

07

AI Strategy & Implementation

From AI readiness to systems in production. We identify where AI genuinely pays inside your operations, then build it: LLM features, retrieval over your own data, and ML models, engineered with the statistical honesty to know when it works and when it does not.

LLMs & RAG · Machine learning · Python · AI readiness

  • AI opportunity assessment

    A ranked map of where AI actually pays in your business, and where it is hype to skip.

  • LLM features & RAG

    Chat, search, summarization and document intelligence grounded in your own data, with guardrails.

  • ML models & evaluation

    Forecasting, classification and scoring models with rigorous validation and monitoring.

08

Websites, Software & Automation

The engineering layer under everything above, and a standalone offering in its own right. Conversion-focused websites, custom web applications, clean APIs, cloud infrastructure and workflow automation, designed and shipped end to end.

React / Next.js · Node.js · Python · AWS / GCP · n8n / Airflow

  • Websites & web apps

    Fast, accessible sites and custom platforms, from dashboards and portals to booking systems, built to convert and built to last.

  • APIs & integrations

    Well-documented APIs and reliable third-party integrations: payments, CRMs, logistics, accounting.

  • Process automation

    Manual hand-offs, reconciliations and reporting turned into monitored, hands-off systems.
