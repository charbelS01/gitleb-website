Home / Process

Four steps, zero surprises.

A predictable path from idea to production. You always know what we're doing, why, and what comes next — no big-bang reveal at the end that misses the mark.

01

Discovery

We immerse ourselves in your business context, goals and constraints. We map the problem before proposing a solution — no assumptions, only clarity before we write a single line of code. You leave this phase with a shared understanding and a clear scope.

Stakeholder interviews Requirements & scope Success metrics
02

Architecture

We design the full system before touching feature code — data models, service boundaries, infrastructure and security. The blueprint is the foundation of everything that follows, and it's where good decisions are cheapest to make. You sign off on the plan before we build.

System design Tech stack decisions Estimates & milestones
03

Engineering

Agile sprints with regular updates and working demos. You see real, deployable progress constantly — not just a status report. Code is reviewed, tested and shipped to a staging environment continuously, so feedback happens early while change is still cheap.

Sprint demos Code review & tests Continuous staging
04

Deploy & Scale

We ship to production, monitor performance and stay on board to ensure your system holds up under real-world load. Handoff includes full documentation and training, so your team can own and extend what we built. We're available for ongoing support if you want it — never required.

Production launch Monitoring & alerts Docs & training
Common Questions

What working with us looks like

With a conversation. You tell us the problem, we ask questions, and we come back with an honest assessment and a proposed scope — usually within a couple of days. No obligation, no hard sell.

Fixed-scope projects are quoted up front against a clear spec. Ongoing work and team augmentation are billed on a monthly retainer. We'll recommend whichever model fits your situation — and tell you if a smaller engagement would do.

Enough to keep us aligned — typically a short weekly check-in plus async updates. We handle the engineering; you provide direction and feedback. We adapt to how your team prefers to work.

You get full documentation, tests and a handoff session so your team can own the system. If you'd like us to stay on for support or new features, we can — but you're never locked in.
